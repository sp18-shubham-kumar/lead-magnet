How to Use This Site
Generating a Cost Comparison Report
  1. Click Start Calculating on the home page.
  2. Choose a Client Location from the dropdown. You need to pick a location first to see available roles in that region (and Spark Eighteen).
  3. Select one or more Roles you want to compare.
  4. Click Verify First and enter your name, email, and (optional) company.
  5. You'll receive an OTP in your email. Enter it to verify.

Requesting New Roles or Locations
  1. If you don't find the role/location you need, click Click here to request it. (Appears at the bottom, After clicking the Start Calculating button in home page.)
  2. Fill out the form with your details and the missing role/location you need.
  3. Click Submit. An OTP will be sent to your email.
  4. Enter the OTP to verify your request.
  5. Your request will be submitted ‚úÖ, and you'll get the final report by email within 48 hours.
